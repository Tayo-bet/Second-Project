About Us

Glory Tabernacle International is a Pentecostal Ministries with a mandate to teach the Glory of God by his word and the Holy Spirit. We believe in the totality of the Word(The Bible) according to the Following Scriptures: Hebrews 11:3 'By faith we understand that the universe was created by the word of God, so that what is seen was not made out of things that are visible' (creation). Hebrews 1:3 'He [the Son] is the radiance of the glory of God and the exact imprint of his nature, and he upholds the universe by the word of his power' (preservation). Hebrews 12:26 'At that time his voice shook the earth, but now he has promised, "Yet once more I will shake not only the earth but also the heavens"' (consummation)
